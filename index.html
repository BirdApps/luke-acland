Specialist Court Advocacy



Luke joined the independent bar after 10 years practice in general litigation. He is an experienced commercial and criminal litigator. At the independent bar, Luke’s speciality is criminal, employment and civil trials. He is reputed for successful alternative dispute resolution

As counsel, Luke has appeared in a number of high profile Nelson cases including allegations of serious criminal offending and significant judicial review cases. Luke regularly advises on civil disputes and often appears in the Employment Relations Authority and Employment Court.

Luke has been a Nelson City Councillor since 2013. He sits on the board of a few local organisations and is engaged with several other groups.
